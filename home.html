About the Project


We make serious games with physical and cognitive challenges for people with disabilities using Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality. Serious games offer conditions for the professional to evaluate and monitor the treatment/training of individuals with disabilities through the performance of motor and/or cognitive tasks in a playful and motivating environment.
