About the Project

We make serious games with physical and cognitive challenges for people with disabilities using Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality. Serious games offer conditions for the professional to evaluate and monitor the treatment/training of individuals with disabilities through the performance of motor and/or cognitive tasks in a playful and motivating environment.

Our Team

We are several health and technology professionals, resulting from a partnership of more than 13 years between the Association in Defense of Inclusion and Parasports for People with Disabilities (ADIPPNE) and, Group of Research and Technological Application in Rehabilitation of School of Arts and Humanities of University of São Paulo (PATER EACH-USP). Here you will find physiotherapists, physical education professionals, team coaches, doctors, occupational therapists, system developers, paratlhetes, inthusiasts, teachers and researchers. All completely passionate about what we do.
